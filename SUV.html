SUV

About: An SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle) is a larger vehicle designed for higher ground clearance and often equipped with four-wheel drive.

Features:

  • Spacious interior with ample seating
  • Ample cargo space
  • Capable of off-road driving
  • Higher driving position
  • Often equipped with advanced safety features
