Hatchback

About: A hatchback is a car with a hatch-type rear door that opens upwards and often features a two-box design.

Features:

  • Flexible cargo space due to fold-down rear seats
  • Usually has three or five doors
  • Combines passenger comfort with cargo-carrying versatility
  • Compact design suitable for urban driving
  • Often more affordable than sedans and SUVs
